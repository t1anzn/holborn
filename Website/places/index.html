Places to Visit in Holborn

Things to Do

Discover the best of Holborn with our curated guide to must-visit spots, hidden gems and unforgettable experiences waiting to be explored.

Swiss Butter

Swiss Butter serves delicious steaks, chicken, and salmon with their signature butter sauces, offering a cozy and flavorful dining experience.

Abeno

Simple Japanese restaurant specialising in savoury pancakes, where tables have embedded hotplates.

The Bountiful Cow

Holborn's hidden gem, The Bountiful Cow is an independent, family-run bar with a delicious steak oriented lunch & dinner menu.

Holborn Dining Room

A grand brasserie, in central London, Holborn Dining Room serves seasonal, locally sourced modern British cuisine with a twist.

Leather Lane Market

Lively weekday street market with stands selling fresh produce, clothing, street food & sundries.

Farringdons Jewellery

Farringdons' growing and ever-changing antique and vintage collection includes exceptional pieces from the Georgian, Victorian, Edwardian and Art Deco eras.

James Smith and Sons

Umbrellas, parasols and walking sticks in handsome, original Victorian shop.

Hunterian Museum

Grisly history of surgery and medicine with gruesome instruments and preserved specimens.

Lucky Voice

Visitors sing karaoke & drink cocktails at this slick operation with private rooms.

Pizza Express Live

Pizza Express Live in Holborn combines great food with live music, offering an intimate venue for jazz, comedy, and more.

Roasting Plant Coffee

Roasting Plant Coffee offers a unique coffee experience with freshly roasted beans on-site, brewed to perfection for every cup.

Kozzy Café

A cozy spot offering hearty breakfast options, delicious sandwiches, and refreshing beverages in a friendly atmosphere.

Blank Street Coffee

A vibrant cafe known for its expertly crafted coffee and inviting atmosphere, perfect for a quick caffeine fix or a relaxed hangout.
