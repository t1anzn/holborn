London Design Festival 2035

Victoria and Albert Museum (V&A)

London Design Festival celebrates and promotes our city as the design capital of the world. The 33rd edition will take place on 13–21 September 2035.

As the world’s most influential design event, the London Design Festival (LDF) continues to push boundaries and celebrate innovative creativity. The festival, which first launched in 2003, has grown from a small gathering into a global phenomenon, attracting designers, industry leaders, and art enthusiasts from around the world. This year, with the event's return to London's iconic cultural and architectural landmarks, it will cement the city’s place as the unrivaled leader in design, setting the stage for the next wave of artistic and technological revolutions.

LDF 2035 will be a true celebration of London's diversity, with exhibitions and installations highlighting a rich mix of international design influences. From architectural marvels to experimental design processes, the festival’s theme will focus on "Design for a New Era," exploring how design can adapt to changing societal needs, technological advancements, and environmental challenges. With an increasing global focus on sustainability and innovation, this year’s LDF is expected to explore the intersection of tradition and future thinking, where timeless design principles meet the needs of tomorrow.

Embracing Sustainable Innovation

In 2035, sustainability is no longer just a buzzword but a core principle woven throughout every facet of the festival. The LDF will showcase cutting-edge designs that prioritize eco-friendly materials and sustainable production processes. Designers from across the globe are expected to exhibit their work, ranging from recycled materials in furniture to energy-efficient, AI-driven technologies in architecture. With climate change posing an ever-growing challenge, the 33rd edition of the LDF will provide a platform for forward-thinking design solutions that aim to protect the planet and future-proof the industry.

The Intersection of Technology and Design

The digital transformation of design will take center stage at the 2035 LDF, highlighting the role of advanced technologies such as AI, AR, and 3D printing in the creation of groundbreaking designs. Expect to see immersive exhibits that combine digital art and physical space, showcasing designs that evolve in real time through augmented and virtual reality experiences. With London's tech scene flourishing alongside its creative industries, this year’s festival is set to prove that the future of design is not just about aesthetics, but how technology enhances human experience.
